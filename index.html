    ABOUT US
    E-CRUSH---PROVISION FOR YOUR VISION
    E-Crush is a student body organisation meant to support students with their english developing skills. It is intended to promote best resources to the students of RGUKT to learn English fluently without hesitation and with confidence. It is the best platform to improve yourself in many ways related to English.. The motto is to help students leave the fear behind and move forward to learn English with love and enthusiasm. Our mission is to make every student and every volunteer to be good in English LSRW(Listening, Speaking, Reading, and Writing) skills. We provide you a platform to learn many helpful things. We spread knowledge of English to all students of the campus by our daily activities, events, sessions, talks and many more.. read more
